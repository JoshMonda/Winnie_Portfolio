About me

Hi, i'm Winnie Tallam Founder of Digital Tech Solution. I've over 3 years of experience in designing and developing websites. I build high-end, user-friendly, and responsive websites with any custom requirements. My aim is to provide my customers with exactly what they want and guide them throughout the process. Lets get in touch and start working on your dream website!

Here are the steps to complete your project:
  1. Website Spec After discussing the project with you i will create a Scope of the Project for your website, including all pages and features.
  2. Final design I'll deliver the final design of your website for your approval. You can review and provide your feedback.
  3. Development I'll implement all the changes provided by you and make it perfect.
  4. Content upload I'll upload your content (ex. text, images, videos, and more) to your website. P.S: I recommend providing the website content at the beginning when the order is started.
  5. Final Review After completing all your requested changes I'll deliver the final version of the website for your review. After you approve the website i will transfer it to your hosting and domain.
