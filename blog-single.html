Website Development Process

  • Dr.Patrick Owoche
  • Development
  • 14

Variety is the spice of life No two projects are the same, even children of the same mother, look different. We owe our success to our tested process that has served us and our clients well across many of website builds.

1. Technical Discovery Assessing the situation, goals, and requirements We begin by assessing your current business surrounding and technology landscape, engaging your key stakeholders and tech leads. Our goal is to understand your business needs, to enable us align technology with your business objectives. If we’re also designing your website, technical discovery is part of an integrated discovery and strategy process.

2. Planning Creating a success guaranteed strategy An optimal technology architecture requires a skillful balancing of factors – current and future needs, workflows, integrations, regulatory requirements, in-house technical resources and, of course, budget – to arrive at the right solution. Our experienced technology team acts as your strategic partners – helping you rebuild your tech stack or finding a solution that plays nicely with the tools you have.

3. Information Architecture and Design Delivering a complete website solution We’re an end-to-end website development agency, so we are more than capable of handling your digital strategy, information architecture, website design, and content creation. We begin planning early for the features we know the site will need, so that we can scope development appropriately. And we architect and design the website with an understanding of the unique capabilities of the platform the site will be built on.

4. front to back end coding Front- and Back-End Coding Our development teams are competent in the whole range of modern web technologies, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Node.js, and Express. With our range of partnerships, we have expertise – including many certifications – for a wide variety of CMS options. Every project is assigned a dedicated technical Project Manager, who will support your equally dedicated Account Director in efficiently managing work quality, time, and budget.

We like to get our clients involved – while the site is in beta, it lives on a development server, where you and your team can play with it, try to break it, and help us eradicate any bugs.

We believe quality is never an accident; it is always the result of high intention, sincere effort, intelligent direction and skillful execution; it represents the wise choice of many alternatives. To ascertain this, we have dedicated teams that ruthlessly evaluate the site against over 100 checkpoints before it even gets close to going live.
